Our Services

Nursing

Our skilled nursing team delivers community-based medical aid, nursing care, and treatment, prioritizing individual choices and addressing the diverse needs of each person with a disability or the elderly.


Nursing Aid

Our Nursing Aid service focuses on personalized attention and assistance. It aims to enhance the overall well-being of individuals, including both those with disabilities and the elderly, through dedicated support, care, and aid.

Medical Center

Medical Centre at Breaking Barriers Disability Services Zimbabwe provides comprehensive healthcare services, including medical check-ups, testing, and rehabilitation.

Assist Personal Activities

Breaking Barriers Disability Services Zimbabwe assists individuals with disabilities and the elderly in various personal activities, promoting independence and ensuring a comfortable and supportive environment.Breaking Barriers Disability Services Zimbabwe proudly offers a comprehensive range of services, including the provision of clothing, meals, and material support. We also focus on the development of our community through the provision of, or facilitation of, training programs for carers and clinicians.

Outreach Programs

Through outreach programs, we extend our support beyond our facilities, providing essential services such as food donations, hot meals, and material support to individuals, including both those with disabilities and the elderly, in the broader community. As part of our commitment to on-site engagement, individuals attending Breaking Barriers Disability Services Zimbabwe have the opportunity to receive hot meals during their visit, emphasizing the importance of community connection and shared support.

Counselling

We provide counselling services to address the holistic needs of individuals, including both those with disabilities and the elderly, and their families, offering emotional and mental health support to navigate challenges and promote overall well-being.
Breaking Barriers Disability Services Zimbabwe is committed to breaking down barriers and creating an inclusive community where individuals with disabilities and the elderly can thrive.
